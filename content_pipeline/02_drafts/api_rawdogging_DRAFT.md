I've never gotten a HubSpot or Salesforce certification and I never will.

I rawdog documentation. Always have.

Certifications are for people who have too much free time. Most of what's in those courses is common sense wrapped in a completion badge so the platform can upsell you on the next tier.

My degree was the same scam. $3,000 for a professor to grade my assignments while a guy in India with a whiteboard in his kitchen on YouTube actually taught me the material. For free. That guy changed how I think. The professor? I don't remember his name.

If your first instinct when something breaks is to open a ticket instead of opening the logs, no cert is going to fix that.

But that's just my opinion.
