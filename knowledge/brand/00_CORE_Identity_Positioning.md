### The Value Proposition
We specialize in **Revenue Extraction and Preservation.** Most businesses are sitting on a goldmine of dormant data. We provide the "Signal" to find it, the "Plumbing" to capture it, and the "Observability" to ensure it never leaks again. This creates the clean data foundation required for the AI-driven economy.

**The Forward Vision:** As AI automates RevOps execution — Clay, HubSpot AI, agent-based workflows — the next bottleneck becomes measurement and accountability. Companies need to know whether their AI is driving efficient revenue outcomes, not just whether systems are running. Inform Growth's Observatory becomes the performance measurement layer for AI-driven revenue motions: the infrastructure that tells you if the automation is actually working.

**The Accountability Angle (Secondary Differentiator):** In a world where any SaaS can be rebuilt quickly and AI executes at machine speed, people don't buy tech — they buy the ability to delegate responsibility. The moat isn't the algorithms or the tooling. It's being the person you can point to when things go wrong. As AI creates an accountability vacuum (automation errors with nobody to fire), Inform Growth is the human layer clients can hold responsible. We don't just monitor systems — we own the problem. "You don't have to think about whether your CRM is healthy. We do." This framing works especially well against the "why not build this yourself?" objection — acknowledge they could, pivot to delegation: "You could build this. But that means you own it."

As AI agents replace human RevOps execution, this layer becomes the performance measurement infrastructure — tracking whether automation drives efficient revenue outcomes, not just whether systems are running.

## 3. Market Position

### Our Position Statement
We are **RevOps Infrastructure Engineers** (operating as "Funnel Plumbers") who prevent data corruption in scaling B2B SaaS & Service companies through continuous monitoring and observability.

### The Market Gap
Most RevOps agencies do one-time fixes: audit, implement, hand off.
**The Problem:** Systems degrade the moment humans start using them.
**The Inform Growth Solution:** We apply infrastructure engineering principles. We don't just fix systems; we build **observability** into them to catch degradation within 24 hours.

### Why Now
AI is automating execution across the RevOps stack — Clay for prospecting, HubSpot AI for workflows, agent-based sequences replacing human SDRs. As execution shifts from human to AI, bad data doesn't slow down the machine — it accelerates mistakes at scale. The demand for engineers who can diagnose, fix, and monitor the infrastructure underneath the AI layer is growing, not shrinking. Inform Growth is positioned as the measurement and accountability layer: solving today's data problems while building the observability infrastructure the AI wave requires.

### Competitive Landscape (The "Us vs. Them")

**Core Positioning:** Every tool in the RevOps stack sits on top of CRM data and assumes it's accurate. Every RevOps agency does a one-time fix and moves on. We go underneath, perform the forensic diagnosis, rebuild the infrastructure, and stay on to make sure it never breaks again.
